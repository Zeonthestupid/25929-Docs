TelemetryUI Lib
===============
TelemetryUI is a dynamic telemetry display system, capable of running functions, controlling parameters, and displaying items on interactive menus.



**Decorated Modules**

These are simply better looking, at the cost of more space or speed of activation. The functionality remains the same regardless of what the decoration adds. This however is with a few exceptions, such as with the dropdowns, which require more inputs, but look better.
